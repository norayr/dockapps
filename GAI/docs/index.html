GAI - The General Applet Interface Library - Programmers guide


Thanks for looking into the programmers reference guide of the GAI library!

The Purpose of the GAI library

To simplify and to give programmers one simple interface for Gnome Panel Applets, ROX panel applets and WindowMaker applets (sometimes called Dockapps) so they can concentrate on what's important, the applet itself. Also the goal is to give the user the flexibility to use the applets as wished. It is planned that GAI will support KDE, XFCE4 and other docks and panels in the future.
